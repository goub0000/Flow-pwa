Data Protection Standards

Flow adheres to the highest international data protection standards, including GDPR, CCPA, and FERPA compliance for educational data.

FERPA Compliance

As an educational platform, we maintain strict compliance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), ensuring student educational records are protected.

  • SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
  • ISO 27001 information security management
  • GDPR compliance for EU users
  • CCPA compliance for California residents
  • FERPA compliance for educational records

Privacy by Design

Our platform is built with privacy as a fundamental principle, not an afterthought. We implement privacy-by-design practices throughout our development process.

  • Data minimization - we only collect what's necessary
  • Purpose limitation - data is used only for stated purposes
  • Transparency - clear communication about data practices
  • User control - you decide how your data is used
  • Security - robust protection measures at every level

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data, with comprehensive audit logs tracking all access.

Role-Based Access

Different user types (students, parents, counselors, institutions) have access only to the data relevant to their role and relationship.

  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Principle of least privilege
  • Regular access reviews
  • Automated deprovisioning
  • Comprehensive audit trails

Data Retention & Deletion

We maintain clear data retention policies and provide easy methods for data deletion when no longer needed.

  • Automatic data expiration based on retention policies
  • Right to deletion upon request
  • Secure data disposal methods
  • Regular cleanup of unnecessary data
  • Clear retention schedules for different data types

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident, we have comprehensive response procedures to protect your data and keep you informed.

Transparency Promise

We commit to transparent communication about any security incidents that may affect your data, including timely notifications and detailed remediation steps.

  • 24/7 security monitoring
  • Rapid incident response team
  • Automated threat detection
  • Regular security drills and testing
  • Clear communication protocols

Your Privacy Rights

You have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data. We make it easy to exercise these rights through our platform.

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to certain processing
