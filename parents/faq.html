Search FAQ

How do I create my parent account? +

To create your parent account, visit the Flow website and click "Sign Up as Parent". You'll need to provide your email, create a password, and verify your identity with your student's information. Once verified, you'll have full access to track your child's application journey.

What information can I see about my child's applications? +

You can view application status, submission deadlines, required documents, payment status, interview schedules, and admission decisions. You'll also receive notifications about important updates and next steps in the application process.

How can I make payments for application fees? +

You can make payments directly through the parent portal using credit/debit cards, bank transfers, or digital wallets. All payments are processed securely, and you'll receive confirmation emails and receipts for your records.

Can I help my child with document uploads? +

Yes! Parents can assist with uploading required documents such as transcripts, recommendation letters, and financial aid forms. However, personal statements and essays should be completed by the student to maintain authenticity.

What browsers are supported? +

Flow works best with modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for optimal performance and security.

How do I reset my password? +

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

When will I know about admission decisions? +

Admission decision timelines vary by institution. You'll receive notifications through the portal when decisions are available. Early decision results typically come in December, regular decision results in March-April, and rolling admissions throughout the year.

Are my payment details secure? +

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and work with certified payment processors. Your financial information is never stored on our servers, and all transactions are protected by multiple layers of security.

Why am I having trouble logging in? +

Common login issues include incorrect email/password, browser cache problems, or expired sessions. Try clearing your browser cache, ensuring caps lock is off, and using the correct email address. If problems persist, use the password reset feature or contact support.

Can I track multiple children's applications? +

Yes! One parent account can be linked to multiple student accounts. You can easily switch between your children's profiles using the student selector in the dashboard to track each child's application progress separately.
