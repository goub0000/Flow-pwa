What information should I include in my support request?

Please include: your institution name, user email, detailed description of the issue, steps to reproduce the problem, browser/device information, and any error messages you're seeing.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Response times vary by priority: General inquiries within 4 hours, Technical issues within 2 hours, Sales inquiries within 1 business day, Emergency issues within 15 minutes.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes! Phone support is available for sales inquiries and emergency technical issues. For general support, we recommend email or live chat for faster resolution.

Can I schedule a demo of Flow?

Absolutely! Contact our sales team at sales@flow.edu or use the "Contact Sales" button above to schedule a personalized demo of Flow for your institution.
