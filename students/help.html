FAQs

How do I submit my application?
Go to Applications, complete the required sections, then click Submit.
Which documents are required?
Typically transcripts, ID/passport, and recommendation letters. See Profile → Documents.
How can I pay fees?
Use Mobile Money, card, or bank transfer in Finance. Payments sync even if you’re offline.
Where can I see messages?
Visit Messages for threads, attachments, and voice notes.
