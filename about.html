About Flow

Transforming African higher education through technology

Our Mission

Flow is dedicated to democratizing access to higher education across Africa by streamlining the university application process. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to pursue their academic dreams, regardless of geographical or economic barriers.

The Challenge

Applying to universities across Africa has traditionally been a complex, fragmented process. Students often struggle with different application systems, varying requirements, and limited access to information about opportunities.

Our Solution

Flow creates a unified platform where students can create one comprehensive profile and apply to multiple African universities seamlessly. We connect students, institutions, counselors, parents, and recommenders in a collaborative ecosystem that supports academic success.
